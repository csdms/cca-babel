Introduction

This chapter provides an overview of the Fortran 90 bindings for SIDL. Common aspects of the bindings, such as the mapping of SIDL data types to their native Fortran 90 representatives, are presented in Section 9.2. Issues of concern to Fortran 90 callers are addressed in the client-side bindings discussion in Section 9.3, while issues of interest to callees of Fortran 90 appear in the implementation-side discussion in Section 9.4.
